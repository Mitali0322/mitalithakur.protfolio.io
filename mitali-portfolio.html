Framework Development

7-Day Content Calendar Framework

Planning Logic

Developed systematic approach to weekly content planning that balances educational value, engagement hooks, and conversion-focused messaging. Calendar structure ensures content variety while maintaining strategic consistency.

Funnel Mapping

  • Top-of-funnel: Awareness content (Monday, Thursday)
  • Middle-of-funnel: Value demonstration (Tuesday, Friday)
  • Bottom-of-funnel: Trust building & CTAs (Wednesday, Weekend)
  • Strategic story sequences for deeper engagement

Audience Targeting

Content timing and format optimized for small business owners and decision-makers. Morning educational posts, midday engagement content, evening value-driven reels based on analytics insights.

Content System

Reel Engagement System

Hook Psychology

Developed library of proven hook patterns for different content types: question hooks for educational content, pattern interrupt hooks for awareness, and curiosity gaps for value demonstration. First 3 seconds optimized for scroll-stopping power.

Content Flow

  • Hook: Attention capture in 2-3 seconds
  • Value delivery: Educational or entertainment core (15-20 seconds)
  • CTA: Clear next action with friction removal
  • Visual pacing optimized for retention

Retention Logic

Strategic use of visual transitions, text overlays, and pacing to maintain viewer attention throughout 30-60 second reels. Content structured to deliver value progressively, encouraging full watch-through.
